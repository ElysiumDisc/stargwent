Custom Font Configuration
=========================

Primary Font: 'Stargate' or similar sci-fi font
Fallback: Arial Bold

Font Sizes:
  - Title: 72pt
  - Subtitle: 36pt
  - Card Name: 18pt
  - Card Power: 24pt bold
  - UI Text: 16pt
  - Small Text: 12pt

To customize: Replace system fonts in main.py with pygame.font.Font('assets/yourfont.ttf', size)
